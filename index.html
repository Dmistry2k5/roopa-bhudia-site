About

London-based, endlessly adaptable, and a pleasure to work with on set.

Roopa is a model and actor based in London with a versatile, non-traditional look that lets her sit naturally across a wide range of briefs and audiences. She moves comfortably between commercial and editorial, beauty, catwalk and bridal.

She has fronted work for brands including Dior, Sky, NatWest and QVC, and has walked more than forty catwalk shows across London and Europe. Alongside modelling she acts, presents and dances, and brings the same steady, professional energy to every job.

Available for shoots, shows, campaigns and events across the UK and beyond.
